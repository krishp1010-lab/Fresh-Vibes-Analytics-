# Fresh-Vibes-Analytics-
A Python-based Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project analyzing 502 YouTube music uploads to uncover business insights and growth strategies for Fresh Vibes Music.
Fresh Vibes Music began during the COVID-19 lockdown as a passion project combining music, video editing, and graphic design. Over five years, the channel grew to 74K+ subscribers and 502 uploads. This project revisits that journey through the lens of data analytics to understand the factors behind successful content and support future business decisions.
